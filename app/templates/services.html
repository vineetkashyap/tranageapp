Driver Training & Certifications

Global Positioning System

The Global Positioning System (GPS) is the tool that allows real time vehicle tracking. It is a blend of more than 27 satellites transmitting signals to receivers around the world. The signals and their interface can make available information about the accurate location of any vehicle fitted out with a GPS receiver. As far as fleet management is concerned, GPS tracking system enables fleet owners and fleet managers to get real-time information about fleet/trucks/vehicles/weather on a map or in the form of reports. These reports can be later cross-checked with other vehicle related information such fuel consumption, driver behavior and maintenance, combining time and position with other data, in order to get real fleet information.
